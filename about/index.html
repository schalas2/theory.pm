About theory.pm

My name is David E. Wheeler, and this is my Perl blog. I’ve been hacking Perl since 1995, when I first started programming. Perl was my first language, and still my favorite. Today I mostly hack on database-related code, including PGXN, a CPAN-like service for PostgreSQL, and Sqitch, a database schema change management app.

I’m @theory on Twitter and also @theory on App.Net.

You can hire me to hack Perl and PostgreSQL via my consulting firm, PostgreSQL Experts.
